Help

Our Community grows larger and larger by the day and we are more than happy to welcome anyone and
everyone into our large family. With that being said, please follow the following guidelines for engaging in
the community.

Guidelines:

  • The first step of joining the community involves creating an account. You only need a username of your
    choice and password to do so.
  • Once an account has been created, please login using your username and password.
  • Once logged in, you will be able to create a new topic to discuss about your issues with our community.
  • Once your topic is created, other members of the community will be able to see your topic and reply to
    provide support.
  • You will also be able to reply to posts and continue the discussion.
  • Please feel free to browse topics created by others in the community as well, to provide support.
  • If you would like to revisit topics created by yourself in the future, click the 'My Topics' menu.
  • Click on the topics menu to sort topics according to three categories - 'Trending, New and Popular'.
  • You can delete your own comments if you believe you have made an error by clocking 'delete' beside a
    comment
